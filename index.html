What is generative AI and how does it all work?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can create entirely new data, like text, images, or even music.  These models are trained on massive datasets, allowing them to learn patterns and use that knowledge to generate content that closely resembles real data.  This makes generative AI a powerful tool for a variety of applications, from creating marketing copy to designing new products.

What is Proof of Physical Work (PoPW)?

In the context of GPU, Proof of Physical Work allows us to complete and authenticate work done within our systems via a transaction hash that can be shared at any time to prove IP addresses our owned physical assets.
